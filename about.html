The Entity Tax Strategy Series

"S Corporation Tax Savings" is the fourth and final book in the AE Tax Advisors Entity Tax Strategy series. Each book in the series provides a comprehensive, practical guide to a specific entity type and the tax strategies available within it.

The series was written to fill a gap we saw in the market: business owners making entity decisions worth tens of thousands of dollars per year based on outdated blog posts or generic advice that fails to account for their specific situation. Together, the four books cover the full landscape of business entity taxation -- partnerships, C Corporations, S Corporations, and real estate investment structures -- giving readers a complete toolkit for optimizing their tax position regardless of which entity type they use.

Why S Corporations Matter

S Corporations represent one of the most powerful and widely used tax strategies for small business owners. The fundamental advantage is simple: by splitting compensation between a reasonable salary and shareholder distributions, S Corporation owners can save thousands of dollars per year in self-employment taxes. But the details matter enormously -- from setting a defensible reasonable salary to optimizing the Section 199A QBI deduction to managing basis for loss deductions.

"S Corporation Tax Savings" covers all of these topics and more, with specific attention to the areas where the IRS focuses its enforcement and where business owners most commonly leave money on the table. The book includes three detailed case studies drawn from real-world client engagements, showing exactly how the strategies work in practice.
